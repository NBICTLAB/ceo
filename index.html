About me

I'm Sadhan Verma,

Hi, thanks for visiting my portfolio. Here is my short biography:


I am a data science enthusiast, educational content creator, infotech instructor, and entrepreneur. I founded North Bengal Information and Communication Technology Laboratory, NBICT LAB at Dinajpur in 2018. I'm also the founder of coursebangla.com, a free e-learning platform.


I have more than five years of experience in the ICT training profession as a trainer. I am also an online instructor at NBICT LAB YouTube channel. In 2020, I trained up the district ambassadors of a2i and ICT4E, who are the teachers from many different schools and colleges of the Rangpur division. I have trained up more than 150 university teachers, about 1000 undergraduate students, about 300 masters & PhD students on SPSS, Excel, R, & Python till today.


I have been running and directing an ICT training project for students and teachers at Hajee Mohammad Danesh Science and Technology University (HSTU), Dinajpur, Bangladesh since 2017. I have completed the Bachelor of Science program from the faculty of Fisheries, HSTU in 2016.
